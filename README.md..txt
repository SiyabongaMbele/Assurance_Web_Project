Project Overview
Assurance is a modern InsurTech platform designed for the South African youth market (Ages 18–30). The site provides simplified insurance solutions for lifestyle assets like laptops, smartphones, and vehicles. Our mission is to promote financial resilience and prevent young professionals from falling into debt cycles.

Website Structure (Sitemap)
The site follows a hierarchical structure with the following pages:
Home (index.html): Brand introduction and hero imagery.
About (about.html): Mission statement and Durban-based history.
Services (services.html): Breakdown of the 6 insurance pillars (Car, Life, Household, Pet, Cell, Laptop).
Knowledge Hub (knowledge-hub.html): Financial literacy resources and debt prevention tips.
Get-a-Quote (get-a-quote.html): Detailed lead-generation form for quotes.
Enquiry (enquiry.html): General contact and support form.

Technical Implementation
Folder Structure: Organized into /css, /images, and /js for clean asset management.
HTML5: Basic semantic structure used for first-year foundation.
Form Logic: Includes demographic tracking (Surname, Address, Work Info) to simulate real-world insurance underwriting.
