About Our Company

Assurance was founded in Durban in 2026 to solve a critical gap in the South African market. We realized that traditional financial safety nets weren't built for the modern realities of students and young professionals, leaving an entire generation exposed to sudden financial setbacks.

Our Mission

We exist to democratize financial resilience for the next generation of South African builders, creators, and leaders.

We believe that an unexpected setback shouldn't derail a lifetime of ambition. Our goal is to replace the anxiety of high-interest debt traps with accessible, transparent protection. By shielding your hard-earned assets—from your first set of wheels to the tools you use to study and work—we empower you to take bold risks, build clean credit histories, and step confidently into your future without fear.
